01

Who we are

WeUp is a personal finance management platform designed for users in Colombia and Mexico. We help you track income, expenses, budgets, and financial goals through an AI-powered dashboard.

This Privacy Policy explains how WeUp collects, uses, and protects your personal and financial information when you use our services at www.weupwealthylife.com and any associated apps.

By using WeUp, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the service.

02

What data we collect

We collect only what we need to provide and improve the service:

Data type What we collect Why
Account info Name, email address To create and manage your account
Financial data Transactions, amounts, categories, descriptions you enter To power your dashboard, reports and AI insights
Preferences Currency, language, income range, financial goal To personalize the experience
Usage data Pages visited, features used, session duration To improve the product
Device info Browser type, OS, IP address For security and fraud prevention

We do NOT collect: bank account numbers, card numbers, PINs, or any payment credentials. WeUp is a manual tracking tool — you enter what you want to share.

03

How we use your data

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the WeUp service
  • Generate personalized AI insights based on your financial data
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • Detect and prevent fraud and security threats
  • Improve our product through aggregated, anonymous analytics
  • Comply with legal obligations in Colombia and Mexico

We will never use your financial data for advertising, profiling for third parties, or any purpose not listed above.

04

Who we share data with

We share your data only with trusted service providers who help us operate WeUp:

Provider Purpose Data shared
Supabase Database & authentication All user data (encrypted)
Google OAuth Optional sign-in with Google Name, email (only if you choose Google login)
Vercel App hosting & delivery Server logs, IP addresses
Anthropic (Claude AI) AI-powered financial insights Anonymized spending patterns (no personal identifiers)

We never sell your data. All service providers are bound by data processing agreements and must handle your information according to this policy.

05

Data security

We take the security of your financial data seriously. Here's how we protect it:

  • AES-256 encryption for all data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit (HTTPS)
  • Row-level security — you can only access your own data
  • Password hashing using bcrypt — we never store plain passwords
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates
  • Multi-factor authentication available for all accounts

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us immediately at [email protected].

07

Cookies

WeUp uses minimal cookies strictly necessary to operate the service:

  • Session cookies to keep you logged in securely
  • Preference cookies to remember your language and currency settings

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics that sell your data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but the app may not function correctly.

08

WeUp is not directed to children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at [email protected] and we will delete the account immediately.
